About
// 01

I'm not trying to be a professional developer, and I'm not chasing a job title. What I'm actually doing is exploring — entrepreneurship, sales, marketing, content creation, psychology, business — and paying close attention to what makes those things actually work, not just how they're supposed to work on paper. I want to build things of my own eventually, not spend my career inside someone else's. Right now that means small projects, service work, reading, testing ideas, and being willing to be bad at something before I'm good at it. I don't have this figured out yet, and I'm not pretending to. This site is less a résumé and more a running log of where I am and where I'm headed.

#003 · PRODUCT · SMART CARDS LIVE

Smart Connect

PURPOSE A QR + NFC smart business card system for local businesses — one tap or scan opens a branded page with their menu, socials, and contact links.
MY ROLE Designed the concept and built the system that spins up a ready-to-deploy card per client: live preview, editable links, a custom QR generator, and one-click deploy to hosting plus an NFC tag. Cafe Upstairs is my demo build to show the concept.
LESSON Systemizing a service into a reusable product beats rebuilding from zero for every client.
